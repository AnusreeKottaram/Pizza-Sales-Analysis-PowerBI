#  Pizza Sales Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes a pizza sales dataset to uncover insights about revenue trends, customer ordering behavior, and product performance using Power BI.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive business view including:
- Monthly revenue trends
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Sales by pizza category and size
- Top-selling pizzas
- Bottom-selling pizzas
- Weekday revenue analysis
- Strategic business recommendations

---

##  Key Insights

- July recorded the highest monthly revenue (72.6K) and highest sales volume (4.4K pizzas).
- November had the highest Average Order Value (AOV), indicating higher-value orders.
- The Classic category is both the most demanded and highest revenue-generating segment.
- Big Meat S is the top-selling individual pizza by quantity.
- Bbq_ckn_s is the least selling pizza by quantity.
- Friday generates the highest revenue among weekdays.
- Large-sized pizzas account for the largest share of total quantity sold.

##  Business Recommendations
- Focus promotional campaigns on Fridays to maximize peak demand.
- Promote high-revenue but lower-demand Chicken pizzas.
- Replicate July-style marketing strategies during slower months.
